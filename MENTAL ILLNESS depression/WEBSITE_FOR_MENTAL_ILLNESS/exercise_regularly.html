Exercise Regularly

It means engaging in physical activity on a consistent and frequent basis as part of a routine or lifestyle. Regular exercise involves participating in activities that increase your heart rate, challenge your muscles, and contribute to overall physical well-being.

. Regular exercise has numerous health benefits, including improving cardiovascular health, maintaining a healthy weight, enhancing mood, and reducing the risk of various chronic conditions.

How will that impact on one's life

Regular exercise can have a positive impact on individuals facing depression. While exercise is not a cure for depression, incorporating it into one's routine can contribute to overall mental well-being.

Engaging in physical activity can lead to an immediate improvement in mood. The sense of accomplishment and the release of neurotransmitters during exercise can counteract feelings of despair and helplessness.

Exercise is known to reduce stress hormones and lower anxiety levels. Regular physical activity provides a healthy outlet for managing stress, potentially decreasing symptoms associated with depression.
